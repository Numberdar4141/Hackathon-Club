About The Hackathon Club

WCTM hacks is a dynamic community of students who are driven by curiosity, creativity, and a desire to tackle challenges using technology. In this club, you'll have the opportunity to participate in hackathons (intense collaborative coding events) and build out-of-the-box projects in a supportive and fun environment.

Themes

Problem statements will be based on following tech domains.

Smart Education

Smart education,a concept that describes learning in digital age. It enables learners to learn more effectively, efficiently, flexibly and comfortably.

Miscellaneous

Technology ideas in tertiary sectors like Hospitality, Financial Services, Entertainment and Retail.

Robotics And Drones

There is a need to design drones and robots that can solve some of the pressing challenges of India such as handling medical emergencies, search and rescue operations.

Smart Automation

Ideas focused on the intelligent use of resources for transforming and advancements of technology with combining the artificial intelligence to explore more various sources and get valuable insights.

Blockchain & Cybersecurity

Provide ideas in a decentralized and distributed ledger technology used to store digital information that powers cryptocurrencies and NFTs and can radically change multiple sectors.

Space technology

Space technology refers to the application of engineering principles to the design, development, manufacture, and operation of devices and systems for space travel and exploration.

Toys And Games

Challenge your creative mind to conceptualize and develop unique toys and games based on our civilization, history, and culture etc.

Tourism

A solution/idea that can boost the current situation of the tourism industries including hotels, travel and others.

Renewable /Sustainable

Innovative ideas that help manage and generate renewable /sustainable sources more efficiently.

Space technology

Space technology refers to the application of engineering principles to the design, development, manufacture, and operation of devices and systems for space travel and exploration.

Sport & Fitness

Ideas that can boost fitness activities and assist in keeping fit.

Disaster Managment

Disaster management includes ideas related to risk mitigation, Planning and management before, after or during a disaster.

Who can participate?

The hackathon Club is open for Students for all branches. It is the right place for anyone who's interested in learning and innovating with their ideas.

01

How much will it cost?

Registration for WCTM Hacks is completely free.

02

How can I apply?

The registration would be simply done by just clicking the register button here and filling up a google form in which you would have to mention you and your team details.

03

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level.

04

What are the prizes to be won?

We are going to announce some really exciting prizes and incentives soon. Stay tuned for further updates!

Can we apply as a team?

Yes! We believe that you’re stronger as a team than apart. You can form teams of 3-4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and development skills.
